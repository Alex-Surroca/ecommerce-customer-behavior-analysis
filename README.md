# E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analysis

##  Project Summary

This project analyzes over 2 million e-commerce transactions to uncover customer purchasing patterns, product demand trends, and reorder behavior.

The goal is to generate actionable business insights that can support decision-making in inventory management, marketing strategies, and customer retention.

---

## Overview

This project explores customer purchasing behavior in an e-commerce dataset using Python for data analysis.

The objective is to identify patterns in customer orders, product popularity, reorder behavior, and purchasing habits over time.

Through exploratory data analysis (EDA), the project aims to generate actionable insights that can support business decision-making in areas such as inventory management, marketing strategies, and customer retention.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains over 2 million records of customer transactions, where each row represents a product within an order.

Key features include:

- Order information (order ID, order sequence, time of purchase)
- Customer identifiers
- Product and department data
- Reorder indicators
- Time between orders

This structure allows for detailed analysis of customer behavior at both the product and order levels.

## Key Insights

- Customer orders peak during mid-morning hours, suggesting alignment with daily routines.
- Fresh grocery products dominate purchasing behavior, indicating recurring shopping patterns.
- High reorder rates reflect strong customer loyalty and predictable demand.
- Staple products such as dairy and fresh produce drive repeat purchases.
- Customers typically purchase multiple items per order, enabling cross-selling opportunities.
- Purchasing behavior follows consistent daily and weekly patterns.

---

##  Business Impact

The findings from this analysis can support:

- **Inventory Optimization** → Ensure availability of high-demand, high-frequency products  
- **Marketing Strategy** → Target promotions during peak purchasing hours  
- **Customer Retention** → Leverage high reorder behavior for personalized recommendations  
- **Revenue Growth** → Increase basket size through cross-selling and bundling strategies
